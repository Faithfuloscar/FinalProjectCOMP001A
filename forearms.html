Forearm workouts

Reverse Grip Wrist Curls

Step:1 Find an appropriate weight take a seat on a flat bench

Step:2 place feet in a stable position to create a vertical shelf for the forearms to rest on

Step:3 Rest forearms on legs with just the bottom of the wrist on the edge

Step:4 ensure the palm of the hand is facing the floor while gripping the dumbbell

Step:5 Raise hands to the top while keeping your arms resting on your legs
