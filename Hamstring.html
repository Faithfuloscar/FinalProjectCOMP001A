Hamstring Workouts

Romanian Deadlifts

Step:1 Ensure your feet are placed shoulder length

Step:2 Grip the bar slightly wider than shoulder length

Step:3 Remove any slack on the bar by pulling on it

Step:4 Squat down while rolling the bar onto your shin

Step:5 lift the bar up utilizing your hips as a hinge movement

Step:6 your starting position is the bar at waist level

Step:7 slowly allow the bar to descend a little past the knees(imagine trying to push your glutes towards the wall)

Step:8 Return to starting position and repeat

Leg Curls

Step:1 Place your hips on the pad so that your knees hang off the edge

Step:2 Place your elbows on the pad to better stabilize the body throughout the movement

Step:3 Place your ankles on the pad connected to the weights

step:4 Curl with your hamstring almost as if you wanted your heel to the sky

Good Mornings

Step:1 Grab the bar with a wide grip and maintain a tight core

Step:2 Un-rack the bar and take three steps back

Step:3 Utilizing only the hip hinge back and lean forward

Step:4 Return to the starting point utilizing the hamstrings
